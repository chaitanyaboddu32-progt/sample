Water Management & Irrigation in Agriculture

Why is Water Management Important?

Proper water management ensures crops get enough water to grow efficiently, helps conserve water resources, and increases agricultural productivity through sustainable practices.

  • Sprinkler Irrigation
  • Surface Irrigation
  • Subsurface Irrigation
